Why DILI Research Matters

Drug-induced liver injury is one of the leading causes of drug attrition during development and post-market withdrawal. Nearly 30% of drug failures in clinical trials are attributed to hepatotoxicity, costing the pharmaceutical industry billions annually and posing severe patient safety risks.

HDAC inhibitors, while revolutionary in cancer therapy, carry a well-documented risk of liver damage. This platform integrates Neo4j knowledge graphs, PubChem molecular data, and AI-driven analysis to help researchers identify hepatotoxicity signals early and make informed decisions in drug development pipelines.
